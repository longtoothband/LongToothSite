Located in Rochester NY, Long Tooth is a Metal band that was formed in 1997 by lead singer Jack Burnett
and bass player Paul Van San.

The Band

Jack Burnett

Lead Singer

Jack is lead and Rhythm guitar he is the lead singer and songwriter of Longtooth.

Jack grew up in a house where each child had to play an instrument. He has been shredding guitar ever since and has been involved in Rochester's metal scene for decades. Jack is definitely the heart of the band with his powerful lyrics to his insane guitar solos he takes his music in an introspective direction.

Paul Van Sant

Bass Player

Paul plays bass guitar for longtooth. Paul started playing the upstate New york thruway circuit in 1984 and has played with Rochester legend SCORCHER.

His talent has reached many cities and locations, even bringing him to the Hollywood strip music scene where he played at iconic places and people. Paul is the energy of the band with his commanding stage presence and his earth shaking bass chord.

AJ Secash

Drummer

Aj is the drummer and percussionist For longtooth. Aj has Played a variety of different projects and collaborations all over Rochester.

He joined Longtooth in early 2024 and has not looked back saying that this is one of his favorite yet challenging projects that he has been involved with. Aj is the vision of the band knowing now with the energy and talent of the group the sky's the limit.
